The Best Movies According To ISHITA

My Top Three Movies of 2022

Pushpa:The rise-Part 1

Its one of the first south Indian Movie that I have watched and I must say that it should be on your watch list. The script, Storyline and acting was all soo awesome. I am eagerly waiting for PART-II.
Rating- 5/5

Drishyam

For those who like crime thriller movies, Drishyam is a perfect choice. It's remake of a malyalam movie by the same name. The film stars Ajay Devgn, Tabu, Shriya Saran, Ishita Dutta and Rishab Chadha. A sequel titled Drishyam 2 was released in 2022.
Rating- 4/5

Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2

Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2 is a 2022 Indian Hindi-language comedy horror film.A standalone sequel to Bhool Bhulaiyaa (2007), the film stars Tabu , Kartik Aaryan and Kiara Advani .follows Ruhaan Randhawa, a rookie psychiatrist who is brought to the Thakur palace to deal with the apparent return of Manjulika, a malevolent spirit, but unwittingly escalates the problems.
Rating- 4.5/5
